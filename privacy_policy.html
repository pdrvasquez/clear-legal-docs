Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Overview

Clear ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Clear mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

All data you enter into Clear, including:

  • Your written thoughts and entries
  • Generated insights and patterns
  • Usage preferences and settings

...is stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms provided by your operating system.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • iOS: AsyncStorage (local device storage)
  • Android: AsyncStorage (local device storage)
  • Web: Browser localStorage

Your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

Clear does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not use cookies, trackers, or any data collection mechanisms.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on:

  • Your device's built-in security features (lock screen, encryption, etc.)
  • Your device's operating system security

We recommend using device-level security features such as passcodes, biometric authentication, and encryption.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the Clear app from your device
  • Clearing app data through your device's settings

Children's Privacy

Clear does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
